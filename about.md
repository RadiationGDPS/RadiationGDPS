# RadiationGDPS

## Details
- Welcome to Radiation Geometry Dash Private Server. This server is dedicated to creators. We host Map pack contests, the top 3 levels get into the map packs. These Creator contests keep our community active. We have a Creators team of our own too, our creators team are dedicated for creating custom main levels, The tower levels, Gauntlets (Renamed to be Elements), and The Map. All the things are still in Work In progress.

## Features
- Official Main levels: Professional musicians remix official RobTop level songs, known as <cg>Radioactive Remixes</c>; these songs get a YouTube album as well. Our creators Construct this Levels with Radioactive theme.
- Elements: Replacement of the gauntlets, known as elements are a series of 5 platformer levels with connected element team, store, lore, vault codes & secrets.
- The tower: Our own take at the tower; Bosses, secrets, vaults & lore.
- The map, The sky, The space: The map is a series of levels you continue after finishing the tower. The sky is our heaven themed reconstruction version of Geometry Dash world levels. The space is a series of levels with Gravity trigger gimmicks.

<cr>Not all of these levels and features are in RGDPS right now, this is a big project and we hope you understand, our creators team are trying their best.</c>
